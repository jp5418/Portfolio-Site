ABOUT JACOB

Jacob Pohl was born, raised and currently resides in the Saint Louis metro area. In 1997, he attended Lewis Univerity majoring in Computer Science. The following year, 1998, Jacob transfered to the Univerity of Missour-Columbia obtaining a BS in Computer Science from the School of Engineering. Upon graduation in 2001, he began his career at SBC (Southwestern Bell Communications) which later merged with AT&T. Over Jacob's 13 year career at AT&T, he has held many technical and leadership positions involving Mainframe Operations, Incident Management, Availability Management and Web Development supporting the Global Technology Operations Center. Jacob is also actively engaged in youth soccer in the Saint Louis Metro area through the Saint Louis Steamers Soccer Club.
